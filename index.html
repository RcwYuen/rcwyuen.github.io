About

I am an undergraduate student pursuing a MEng in Computer Science at University College London (UCL). I am interested in Machine Learning and its application in biomedicine. To pursue this interest, I am planning to take modules such as Bioinformatics, nanotechnology or biomedical engineering from UCL's IEP Minor. I am interested in applying Bioinformatics, Machine Learning Models, nanotechnology and medical practice in nanobots for minimally invasive (neuro)surgeries or nanobots in the circulatory system for immunology and body regulation.

I have been working in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) for the three consecutive summers from 2018 as an intern. In these three years, I have been working in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering under their Swire and Chair Professor, Professor Xin Zhang. I have developed a software system for laboratory equipment logging, have participated and assisted in the preparation of postgraduate and research experiments.

During the summer of 2020, I have relished an opportunity to work in the Biointelligence Laboratory of the Big Data Institute in HKUST under the supervision of Professor Wang Yang, Dean of Science & Chair Professor of Mathematics and Professor Yang Can, Assistant Professor of Mathematics. I am working on a machine learning project under their supervision.

Other than these opportunities, I have written an essay on FinTech entitled 'A Comparative Study of the Performance of LSTM in Forex and Stock Market Time Series Forecasts' which has been modified from my GCE A-Level Computer Science coursework entitled 'An Empirical Study of LSTMs in Forex Market Predictions'. I have completed this project without supervision in the academic year of 2019 to 2020. I have also written an essay to investigate the relationship between Artificial Intelligence and Medicine for an essay prize.

My main interests include Bioinformatics, Machine Learning, Biomedical Image Processing, Deep Learning and Neural Networks.
